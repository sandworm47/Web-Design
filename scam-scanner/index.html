How does it work?

Every item sold on Amazon has at least 3 identifying serial numbers. Amazon uses these serial codes to track no only the exact product, but that product line. By using Amazon's shared database, we see what Amazon sees. Through these serial numbers, Amazon's database knows where it came from, and because of the product type, who it came from. Thankfully for us, Amazon has already done all the work, we are just sifting through the fruit of thier labors. Big preesh Jeff.
